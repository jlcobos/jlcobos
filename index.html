    Services

    First, the secret sauce...

    I keep my pricing low by using professionally designed templates just like this very site. By understanding the code with which these templates are built, I am able to customize each one to fit of your small business needs. Just a few modifications to a templates color scheme, layout, font, and images can completely change it's appearance to the point that the original is unrecognizable. What many non programmers don't know, is that many of the features you see on this site are built using pre written code called code "libraries". By leveraging these free and widely available libraries, development time an costs can be drastically reduced. This can be the difference between paying just a few hunderd dollars or thousands for a website. Unless you need a highly customized website with enterprise level features such as credit card payments, you can dramatically reduce your costs by leveraging templates and code libraries. Depending on your needs, I can also help you find reliable and low cost website hosting services.

    Offerings

    Landing Pages

    Simple one page websites. This option may be ideal for businesses websites that do not require much content. Most or all content is visible on the screen with minimal or no scrolling necessary.

    Multi-Page Websites

    If your small business website needs to display a large amount of informaton that needs to be segregated, this may be the best option.

    Complex Single Page Websites

    If the content of your website does not require significant segregation, this may be an ideal option. An advantage is that your site visitors will not need to navigate back and forth between pages, which eliminates the loading and reloading of the individula site pages.

    Mobile Responsiveness

    For years, the need for websites to work seamlessly on mobile devices has been a given. Most of the templates I use are already mobile friendly or can be easily made to be mobile friendly at no extra cost.
